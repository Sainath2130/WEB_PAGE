About Our Divine Journey

Shree Shivkrupanand Swami's life is a testament to unwavering devotion and selfless service. From a young age, his profound spiritual quest led him through rigorous meditation and ascetic practices, culminating in deep realization and a commitment to uplift humanity. His journey, marked by humility and immense grace, has inspired millions to seek inner peace and contribute positively to the world.

The Gurukarya, or Guru's work, is a sprawling endeavor of spiritual and humanitarian initiatives. It encompasses the establishment of meditation centers, ashrams, and various service projects aimed at fostering spiritual awareness, promoting health, and supporting community welfare. Each step of this journey reflects a profound dedication to spreading universal love and harmony.

Milestones & Ashrams Established

Early 1980s: The Genesis of Sadhana

Intense spiritual practices and realization of universal truth, laying the foundation for Gurukarya.

Late 1990s: First Ashram & Community Initiatives

Establishment of the first Ashram, becoming a hub for meditation and charitable activities. Expansion of community welfare programs.

2000s Onwards: Global Outreach & New Centers

Rapid expansion with numerous ashrams and meditation centers established across India and internationally, reaching diverse communities.
